What is a magic square?

A magic square is the arrangement of numbers in such a way that the sums of all rows, columns and two main diagnols are equal.It is often arranged in the form of a grid and each box of the grid is filled with integers so that at the end you will get a matrix in which the sum of each row,sum of each column and sum of each diagnol is a constant.A typical magic square looks like the one in below.
